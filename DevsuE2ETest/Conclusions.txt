"One of the most challenging aspects of the technical exercise was dealing with popups (alerts) that appear when adding a new product. Managing these alerts proved intricate, as Serenity lacked a specific method for handling the driver in this scenario. To overcome this hurdle, a custom method was meticulously crafted using Selenium. This method not only provides control over the alert but also allows the extraction of its text. With this capability, the test can now interact intelligently by clicking the appropriate button within the alert, ensuring a more robust and dynamic test execution process
